Help/FAQ


Is this mobile app only targetted for UCSD International Students?

No! Non-international students are encouraged to create a profile. The features of this app such as navigate, plan, and connect are great ways for new students to become integrated on campus. Additionally, bilingual students or students seeking to tutor or become acquainted with a second language can use this social app to meet international students on campus.

How can I get a Social Security Number(FF1 student)

You must apply in person at a local Social Security Administration office (SSA). F-1 students must provide the following documents to apply for a SSN: Job offer letter from your employer with a start date no more than 30 days in the future Verification letter from the UCSD International Center. Please complete and submit a Letter Request Form and your job offer letter from your employer to obtain a Verification letter. Verification letters take 5 business days to process. Passport I-94 card (small white card stapled in the passport) NOTE: Copy the front and back of the I-94 to include with your application. How do I take a leave of absence?

Students who wish to go on a leave of absence from UC San diego for one or more quarters must obtain permission from the International Students & Programs Office prior to their proposed leave. Both F-1 and J-1 students can find out how to do this on our Leave of Absence page.
